Background

This app is using the IBM dataset which comprises 10 columns and 14999 observations of HR data for a fictional company. The dataset has 11428 observations of people that stayed with the company and 3571 observations that have left. This app follows a pretty normal process of loading our data, using decriptive stats to evaluate the headline findings and then we visualise to gain a deeper understanding of our datset. Once we have a good feeling of our data, we begin to select and train our models. As we are trying to understand if someone will leave or not (0 or 1), we are selecting our model from the classification branch of models. We will be evaluating and trainig a Logistic Regression and then a Random Forest and comparing them. We will then deploy the app using flask, some simple HTML for our home page and CSS for our styling.
